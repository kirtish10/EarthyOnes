CTE / NOC

Consent to Establish / No Objection Certificate

It involves making an application in a prescribed format to respective State Pollution Control Board along with required documents and scrutiny fees.

It is followed by physical inspection of the site and assessment of the environmental management system proposed so as to meet with the mandatory requirement prescribed by State pollution Control Board.

CC&A / CTO

Consolidated Consent And Authorization / Consent to Operate

Before starting / operating the activity. After getting NOC from the Board, the unit can start work related to start up the activity.

And before operating the activity, the permission in the form of Consolidated Consent & Authorization [CCA] [Consent to Operate] has to be obtained, under Water Act-1974, Air Act-1981 and Environment Protection Act, 1986 and its amendment.
