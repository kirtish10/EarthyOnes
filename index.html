ETP PLANT

An ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) is a system that is designed to treat the wastewater generated by industrial and commercial operations before it is discharged into the environment.

STP PLANT

STP (Sewage Treatment Plant) treats wastewater from residential, commercial and industrial sources to remove harmful substances, pathogens and pollutants using physical, biological and chemical processes.

Read More

ELECTRIC EVAPORATOR (ZLD)

An electric evaporator is a device that uses electricity to evaporate water from a wastewater stream in a Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) system, leaving behind solid waste for disposal.

SCRUBBERS

Scrubbers are devices used in industrial processes to remove harmful gases or particles from exhaust streams. They work by passing the stream through a liquid or solid material to capture and remove pollutants.
